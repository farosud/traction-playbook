# Social & Display Ads — Traction Channel 5

## Service Layer
- Exa.ai: research competitor ad strategies, find ad copy examples
- Replicate/FAL.ai: generate ad creative images
- Serper: research trending topics to ride with ads
- SociaVault: analyze competitor social presence for targeting ideas
- Firecrawl: scrape competitor landing pages

## On Invocation

### 1. Gather Context
- Product + audience (B2B, B2C, technical, creative, etc.)
- Monthly ad budget ($100, $500, $1K, $5K+)
- Goal: awareness, signups, trials, purchases?
- Existing social presence? (which platforms, follower count)
- Any past ad experience? What worked/failed?

### 2. Platform Selection
Score each platform for THIS product:

FORMAT:
  PLATFORM: [name]
  AUDIENCE FIT: [1-10]
  TYPICAL CPC: [$X.XX range]
  MIN VIABLE BUDGET: [$X/day]
  BEST FOR: [awareness / signups / demos / purchases]
  TARGETING STRENGTH: [what you can target precisely]
  CREATIVE FORMAT: [image, video, carousel, text]
  RECOMMENDATION: [TEST / SKIP / LATER]
  WHY: [specific reason for this product]

Platforms to evaluate:
- Facebook/Instagram (Meta Ads)
- LinkedIn Ads
- Twitter/X Ads
- Reddit Ads
- TikTok Ads
- Google Display Network
- YouTube Ads
- Pinterest Ads (if relevant)

Recommend TOP 2-3 platforms to test. Not all of them.

### 3. Targeting Strategy
For each recommended platform:

  AUDIENCE 1 (interest-based):
    Interests: [list]
    Demographics: [age, location, job title if applicable]
    Estimated reach: [range]

  AUDIENCE 2 (lookalike/similar):
    Based on: [existing users, email list, website visitors]
    How to create: [steps]

  AUDIENCE 3 (retargeting):
    Who: [website visitors, free trial users, abandoned signup]
    Pixel/tracking setup needed: [yes/no + how]

### 4. Creative Concepts
For each platform, generate 3-5 ad creative concepts:

FORMAT per creative:
  CONCEPT NAME:
  FORMAT: [image / video / carousel / text]
  HOOK (first 3 seconds or first line): [what stops the scroll]
  MESSAGE: [core value proposition]
  CTA: [specific call to action]
  VISUAL DESCRIPTION: [what the image/video looks like]
  COPY (full ad text):
    Primary text: [main body]
    Headline: [short]
    Description: [if applicable]

Creative categories:
- Problem-agitation: show the pain, then the solution
- Demo/product: show the product working
- Social proof: quote/testimonial
- Contrarian: challenge a common belief
- Educational: teach something, mention product naturally
- UGC-style: casual, phone-recorded feel

### 5. Budget Allocation
- Split budget across platforms (60/40 or 70/30 to start)
- Daily budget per campaign
- How long to run before judging results (minimum 7 days, ideally 14)
- When to shift budget (which metrics trigger reallocation)

### 6. A/B Testing Framework
Test one variable at a time:
- Week 1-2: Test hooks (3 different opening lines/images)
- Week 3-4: Test audiences (2-3 targeting approaches)
- Week 5-6: Test CTAs (different offers/actions)
- Week 7+: Scale winners

### 7. Kill Criteria
Per platform:
- If CPC > $[X] after $[Y] spent → pause
- If CTR < [X]% after [N] impressions → change creative
- If no conversions after $[X] → kill platform
- Compare CPA across platforms → consolidate to winner

### 8. Retargeting Strategy
People who visited but didn't convert:
- What ad to show them (different from cold audience)
- Frequency cap (don't annoy)
- Time windows (1 day, 3 day, 7 day, 30 day)
- Sequential messaging (show different ads over time)

### 9. Measurement
- CPC, CTR, CPM by platform
- Conversion rate by creative
- CPA by audience
- ROAS
- Frequency (how often same person sees ad)
- Platform comparison dashboard
