# Search Engine Marketing (SEM) — Traction Channel 4

## Service Layer
- Serper: keyword research, SERP analysis, competitor ad copy analysis
- Exa.ai: find content ranking for target keywords, understand search intent
- Firecrawl: scrape competitor landing pages for copy inspiration

## On Invocation

### 1. Gather Context
- Product + what it does
- Who is the customer? (B2B, B2C, technical, non-technical)
- What problem does it solve? (in the customer's words, not yours)
- Monthly budget for ads ($100, $500, $1K, $5K+)
- Any previous SEM experience? (if yes, what worked/didn't)

### 2. Keyword Strategy

#### Problem Keywords (highest intent, often cheapest)
Search terms people use when they have the PROBLEM your product solves.
They don't know your product exists. They're Googling the pain.

Generate 20-30 problem keywords:
  "[how to] + [problem]"
  "[problem] + [solution type]"
  "[frustration] + [industry/role]"

#### Solution Keywords (direct intent)
People searching for the TYPE of solution you are:
  "[product category] + tool"
  "[product category] + for [audience]"
  "best [product category] + [year]"

#### Competitor Keywords (comparison intent)
People searching for competitors or alternatives:
  "[competitor] + alternative"
  "[competitor] + vs"
  "[competitor] + review"

#### Long-Tail Gold (low CPC, high conversion)
Very specific queries with low competition:
  "[specific use case] + [tool/solution]"
  "[specific role] + [specific problem] + [help/tool]"

### 3. Ad Copy Generation
For the top 10 keyword groups, write:

Google Ads format:
  HEADLINE 1 (30 chars): [primary hook]
  HEADLINE 2 (30 chars): [value prop]
  HEADLINE 3 (30 chars): [CTA or differentiator]
  DESCRIPTION 1 (90 chars): [expand on problem + solution]
  DESCRIPTION 2 (90 chars): [social proof or urgency]
  DISPLAY URL PATH: [keyword-relevant path]

Write 3 variations per keyword group for A/B testing.

### 4. Landing Page Recommendations
For each keyword group, the landing page should:
- Mirror the search intent exactly (message match)
- Lead with the problem, not the product
- Show the solution in action (screenshot, demo, video)
- Single clear CTA
- Load fast (< 3 seconds)
- Mobile-first

### 5. Budget Allocation
Given the total budget:
- Split across keyword groups by estimated ROI
- Recommend daily spend per campaign
- Set bid strategy (manual CPC to start, switch to target CPA after data)
- Define test period (typically 2-4 weeks for statistical significance)

### 6. Negative Keywords
Generate a negative keyword list to prevent wasted spend:
- Free (if not offering free tier)
- Jobs/careers/salary (unless hiring)
- Tutorial/course (unless selling education)
- Industry-specific irrelevant terms

### 7. Kill Criteria
When to stop:
- CPC > [threshold] for [time period]
- CTR < 1% after 1000 impressions
- No conversions after $[X] spent
- CPA > [X]% of customer LTV

### 8. Scaling Plan
If the test works:
- Expand to more keyword groups
- Test responsive search ads
- Add retargeting campaigns
- Explore Microsoft/Bing Ads (cheaper, same format)
- Consider Performance Max campaigns

### 9. Measurement
- CTR by keyword group
- CPC trends
- Conversion rate by landing page
- CPA (cost per acquisition)
- ROAS (return on ad spend)
- Quality Score improvements over time
